Color

Brand

Our brand color is called Triforce, inspired by the golden triangles of its namesake. Triforce is a very bold, intense colour that contracts gorgeously against the almost-blue darkness of the vertical color stack we use to imply the depth of components.

Vertical colors

Since ZSR prefers a dark theme for all content, we have borrowed a page from Material Design. To make sure our pages remain easily viewable and do not become distracting, we use a concept of a vertical stack. The more stacked up a component is, the lighter the color becomes. We've chosen a very deep, rich base that is almost black, but has just the slightest hint of blue, like peering into a very, very deep lake. The closer the components get, the more it looks like the water is becoming shallow. We call this color stack Lake Hylia, after the famous, recurring lake in Hyrule.
